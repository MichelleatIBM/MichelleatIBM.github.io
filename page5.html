Smarter Schools Event

This event is open to Year 8 students.  The aim is to encourage their interest in STEM and show them the career opportunities available within a technology company such as IBM. 

The event is in two parts. In an ideation workshop at your school, your students are challenged to look at ways in which their school could be more sustainable. The two teams with the best ideas are invited to IBM Hursley for a Grand Finale, and the chance to compete for the top spot.

Ideation workshop
A half day workshop run at your school on a date of your choice. The workshop introduces students to IBM, and then in teams of four, they brainstorm ways their school could be more sustainable, using technology. 

Grand Finale
The teams with the best ideas from each school workshop are invited to a day at IBM Hursley, where they will be inspired by our innovation and technology with demos and tours on site. They will present their solutions in an expo where they will compete to be chosen winners.
