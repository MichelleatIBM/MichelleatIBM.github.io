Work Experience

Student placements at IBM will help you gain a better understanding of the world of work. You will be given an opportunity to work on projects alongside IBM employees and you'll get an appreciation of a typical working week.  We currently offer two types of work placements; Software Development and Design.  

Read on to find out what some of our recent students thought of their placements and how you can apply....

Software Development

Do you have a logical mind? A placement in a software development team will open your eyes to a coding world. You don't need previous experience, just a passion for technology. Note that the majority of software development placements are technical and computer based. 

Design

Are you a keen artist with a passion for technology? The Hursley Studio has placements to offer for people like you. You'll learn about the Design Thinking methodology and work with a variety of mentors through your project.

Application process

1

Application deadline

Applications are open in the autumn term and students are encouraged to apply as early as possible to avoid disappointment.                                             

2

What to include

Make sure to include the subjects you are studying, why you would like the placement and what you hope to achieve.                                                             

3

Communication

You will be contacted via email to confirm whether or not you have secured a placement. Further information will be sent a few weeks prior to your start date.
