School Visits

Primary Schools

Aimed at year 5 and 6 students, a visit to Hursley will give you a peak behind the scenes and show you what we do at Hursley.
Groups of up to 30 students visit at a time and typically spend around 3 hours with us.

A typical visit includes:
A introduction to IBM and Hursley
Machine learning hands-on activity
Tour of the site
Visits to some of the following:
   The IoT Lab
   The Emerging Technology Lab
   The Hursley Museum
   The Maker Space

Secondary Schools

Visits to IBM Hursley will expose students to the technology we work with through tours of our demo labs and site, and the opportunity to meet software developers, designers and some of our master inventors. 

Students can meet with apprentices, interns and graduates to better understand what it's like to work in IBM, the roles available and the process to follow if they are interested in joining after sixth form or university.
